# Atlas of Canada

![Atlas of Canada Banner](./img/atlas_banner.png)

## Discover Canada through geography

The Atlas of Canada provides interactive and static maps of Canada, from past to present. Find, explore and download a variety of maps and discover other sources of Canadian geographic information.

## Overview

The **Atlas of Canada** is the official atlas of Canada, providing comprehensive geographic information and mapping resources for understanding Canada's landscape, people, and resources. This digital atlas combines historical cartographic knowledge with modern web mapping technologies to deliver an engaging and informative experience.

## Key Features

- **Interactive Maps**: Explore dynamic maps with layers covering physical geography, demographics, economy, and more
- **Historical Archive**: Access digitized versions of atlas editions dating back to 1906
- **Thematic Collections**: Browse curated map collections on topics like climate, natural resources, and population
- **Educational Resources**: Utilize maps and data for teaching and learning about Canadian geography
- **Data Downloads**: Export map layers and geographic datasets for analysis

## Map Collections

The Atlas of Canada includes comprehensive thematic map series:

### Physical Geography
- Topography and landforms
- Climate and weather patterns
- Freshwater resources
- Coastal and marine environments

### Human Geography
- Population distribution and demographics
- Indigenous territories and languages
- Urban development and infrastructure
- Economic activities and resources

### Environmental
- Ecosystems and biodiversity
- Protected areas and parks
- Environmental changes and monitoring
- Natural hazards and risks

## Historical Atlas Editions

Explore the evolution of Canadian cartography through digitized atlas editions:

- **First Edition (1906)** - Canada's inaugural national atlas
- **Second Edition (1915)** - Expanded coverage during WWI era
- **Third Edition (1957)** - Post-war modernization
- **Fourth Edition (1974)** - Comprehensive update
- **Fifth Edition (1978-1995)** - Multi-volume series
- **Sixth Edition (1993-present)** - Digital transformation

## Technical Information

The Atlas of Canada leverages modern web mapping technologies:

- **GeoView Platform** - Built on the Canadian Geospatial Platform's viewer framework
- **Open Standards** - Uses OGC-compliant services (WMS, WFS, GeoJSON)
- **Responsive Design** - Accessible on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Bilingual** - Available in both English and French
